Mango Madness

Imagine you’re in a desert town at the turn of the millenia in the year 1000. You’ve traveled many miles, and your tongue is as dry as sandpaper. You haven’t drunk water in two days, and you can feel your throat starting to lose hope. But you turn a corner, and a woman in a purple gown ushers you to a marketplace, and you can’t believe it isn’t a mirage. Mangoes - thousands of them - with glorious moisture and bursting with flavor!

Kiwi Karma

You’re sitting in traffic, just trying to get home after a long day at work. A millennial in a bright red Mustang cuts you off - clearly having zero respect for the rule of law or the well-being of the community. However, you see an enormous kiwi rolling down the side of the hill towards the Mustang, and it takes the millenial out. Boom! Kiwi Karma.

Applepocalypse

That Tuesday started off like any other. You forgot your school lunch at home, and you got called to the office when your parents dropped it off. Your whole class murmured “Ooooooh” thinking that you were in trouble. But really, everyone was in trouble. Right as the bell for recess rang, apples began to fall from the sky. Applepocalypse!

Blueberry Bash

It’s 2007. Taylor Swift is still leading the Top 40. Your friend invites you to a party in her friend Jeff’s apartment, which is a little small but should be fine for a small gathering like this. You dress up as you normally would, but upon entering, everyone turns to you in disgust at your button-up shirt and khakis. “Didn’t you get the memo?” Jeff says, “It’s a Blueberry Bash!” You realize in embarrassment that they’re all wearing blueberry suits - like the one from Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.

Raspberry Rumble

California has always been prone to earthquakes, but you weren’t expecting this when you moved to San Francisco with your college boyfriend back in ‘98. Glasses shatter and plates break as your entire apartment building shifts back and forth. You seek cover in the shelter downstairs, and your neighbor offers you a package of soothing raspberries. “Raspberries?” you ask. “Why now?” “I don’t know -” She responds, “It’s just this tradition we’ve had in this building forever. We call it the Raspberry Rumble.”

Straw-banana Supernova

In a place before time and space, there was a single strawberry with the mass of the entire universe. A banana grew from it. Their masses were then so large that they began to orbit one another at the greatest speed ever measured. They crashed into one another, somehow creating a supernova and giving birth to all things. Accordingly, scientists have called it the Strawberry Banana Supernova to this day.

Coconut Calamity

A celebrity chef investigates your store, and finds a stash of coconuts that you completely forgot about. You curse under your breath - you know they’re rotten. He turns to you in disgust “This is a Coconut Calamity!”

Starfruit Surprise

It’s your first time in Southeast Asia. You’re supposed to meet your distant relative at this cafe, but a shady man whisks you away to a room in the back. “You wanna try some of this?” he says. He turns around and offers you a ripe starfruit.

Berry Blast

Everyone’s forgotten about your birthday. No one at work seemed to remember it, and even your wife hasn’t mentioned it at all, much less your kids. You start to question your life decisions. But then you get home, and the lights turn on all of a sudden. “Surprise!” all of your closest friends and family scream. They smack a plateful of berries into your face in celebration. (It would be a cake, but you were diagnosed with lactose intolerance three years ago).
